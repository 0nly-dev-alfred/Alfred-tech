The Visionary

As a specialist in Full-Stack development and digital systems, Samuel has spent years mastering the intersection of hardware logic and software execution. From his base in Ibadan, he has built a reputation for creating high-performance digital solutions.

The Origin Story

The Ibadan Midnight

The story of this academy began long before the first student enrolled. It began with Adejumo Samuel Ademola hunched over an HP laptop, navigating the friction between ambition and limited resources. In the quiet hours of 2:00 AM in Ibadan—long after the city noise had faded—Samuel was self-teaching the complexities of JavaScript, the precision of Photoshop, and the logic of audio engineering.

"I realized that the gap between a student and an expert isn't talent—it’s the willingness to stay awake and keep clicking 'Refresh' until the code finally turns green."

Every technical hurdle—from debugging complex modules to troubleshooting hardware—became a brick in the foundation of Alfred Tech. Samuel built this academy to provide the exact roadmap he wished he had: a path forged in grit, refined by experience, and designed for those ready to lead the tech revolution.
